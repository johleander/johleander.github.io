My name is Johannes Leander.
I’m an UI/UX engineer who enjoys the challenge of creating advanced applications with the end user in mind.
As I´ve never been able to decide whether I prefer the technical or creative aspect of product development I tend to do a bit of both.
My passions lie in taking technical requirements and convert them into an easy-to-use application and I strongly believe that the user experience is equally important as the technical implementation.

When I´m not creating applications or improving user experiences I like to spend time with my family, watch a soccer game or trading some stocks.
